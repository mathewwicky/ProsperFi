Get to know about our company

Prosper Finance was founded in 2005 as the first peer-to-peer lending marketplace in the United States. Since then, Prosper Finance has facilitated more than $25 billion in loans to more than 1,400,000 people.
Through Prosper Finance, people can invest in each other in a way that is financially and socially rewarding. Borrowers apply online for a fixed-rate, fixed-term loan between $1,000 and $100,000. Individuals and institutions can invest in the loans and earn attractive returns. Prosper handles all loan servicing on behalf of the matched borrowers and investors. Prosper Marketplace is backed by leading investors including Francisco Partners, Institutional Venture Partners, Third Point, and Jefferies.

Prosper Finance wasn't born to be another faceless lender. We came into the world driven by a single purpose: to empower individuals and businesses with fair, flexible financing tools. Leveraging technology and a team of dedicated experts, we've built a global network serving countless dreams. From debt consolidation to business expansion, we've helped unlock potential across borders and backgrounds. Our journey doesn't end here. We're constantly innovating, pushing boundaries, and striving to ensure financial empowerment reaches every deserving hand. Join us as we turn ambitions into reality, one loan at a time.
